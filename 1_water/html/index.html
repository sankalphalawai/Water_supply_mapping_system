Problem Statement ID: 1423

Title: Enhancing Water Supply Network Mapping

Description: The Jal Jeevan Mission aims to deliver clean drinking water reliably to every rural household. To achieve this vision, we require an innovative, cost-effective technology solution. Our goal is to develop a web/mobile-based tool for mapping water supply networks. This tool will create a comprehensive geospatial database of critical water supply infrastructure, incorporate grievance redressal mechanisms, and an alert monitoring system.

Organization: Ministry of Jal Shakti
